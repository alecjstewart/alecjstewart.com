About Me

My name is Alec Stewart, and I like to make things, I often break things, and sometimes I take things too seriously. I am fascinated by the capabilities of technology and what we can do with them, but I can't dismiss their darker consequences.

When I'm not sitting behind a screen, I like to read and write for pleasure and to learn. I also spend far too much time thinking about how to be a better human and what makes life meaningful.

This website is a culmination of my interests, but mostly it's my central hub for the interesting tidbits I feel are worth sharing with the world.
